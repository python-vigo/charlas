Employee Benefits Policy
TechNova Inc. offers employees 15 paid vacation days per year. 
Sick leave is separate and provides up to 10 paid sick days annually.
Employees must submit leave requests through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance.

Product Warranty Information
TechNova Inc. provides a 2-year limited warranty on all electronic devices.
Warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not include accidental damage.
Customers can file warranty claims through our online portal or by visiting authorized service centers.

IT & Security Policy
Employees must use multi-factor authentication (MFA) to access company systems.
Company Wi-Fi credentials are confidential. The guest Wi-Fi password is 'TechNovaGuest2024'.
Employees must report any security breaches to IT support immediately.

Customer Support Guidelines
TechNova Inc. guarantees a 24-hour response time for all customer inquiries.
Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase if the product is returned in its original condition.
Technical support is available via live chat, email, and phone from 9 AM - 6 PM (Monday-Friday).

Office Location & Work Policy
TechNova Inc. headquarters is located at 123 Innovation Drive, Silicon Valley, CA.
Office hours are from 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees have the option to work remotely twice a week, subject to manager approval.
